Common Enquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Find immediate answers to questions about accepted scrap items, weighting protocols, and pickup logistics.

What scrap do you buy?
We purchase a broad spectrum of scrap materials including Base Metals (Iron, Steel, Copper, Aluminum, Brass), Electronics & E-waste (Laptops, CPUs, monitors, wiring, TVs), old Household Appliances (ACs, Fridges, Microwave, Washing Machines), Paper & Cardboard, Scrap Batteries, and Scrap Vehicles (Cars & Motorcycles).
Do you provide doorstep pickup?
Yes, we provide same-day doorstep collection services across Pondicherry & Kottakuppam. Our logistics team will bring weighing scales and handling vehicles right to your residential door or commercial warehouse.
How is pricing calculated?
Pricing is calculated based on the net weight of the materials multiplied by the daily market rate of that specific category. We utilize highly precise, ISO certified digital scales to ensure 100% honesty and accuracy.
Do you buy old electronics?
Absolutely. We buy computer towers, laptops, old screens, servers, computer peripheral cards, office wiring, and telecommunications equipment. All e-waste is broken down safely matching green recycling regulations.
Can I sell damaged appliances?
Yes, we purchase non-working, damaged, and old appliances. Items like air conditioners, refrigerators, and washing machines have high metallic recovery values even if the electronic components no longer function.
How quickly can pickup be arranged?
Once you confirm your quotation and schedule a pickup, we aim to dispatch our pickup truck on the same day, typically within 2 to 4 hours, depending on your location and route availability.
